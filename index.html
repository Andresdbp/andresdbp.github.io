Current & Past Projects

Exploring genome evolution and population genetics through computational approaches

Current

SeqDef: Phylogenetically Weighted Genomic Prioritization

R package for phylogenetically weighted genomic prioritization across the tree of life. Enables researchers to identify and prioritize genomic regions based on evolutionary context.

R Phylogenetics Bioinformatics
Current

Epistatic Capacitance

Understanding the accumulation of epistatic variations in populations and its effect on future adaptation. Developing theoretical framework for cryptic genetic variation.

Population Genetics Simulations Python
Published

Evolution of Achiasmatic Meiosis

Exploring evolutionary dynamics of achiasmatic meiosis, investigating drivers including sexual antagonism versus heteromorphy-dependent aneuploidy.

Sex Chromosomes Meiosis Modeling
Current

Self-Compatibility and Karyotype Evolution

Investigating how self-compatibility weakens barriers to karyotype change and increases chromosome evolution rate in angiosperms.

Chromosome Evolution Plant Biology
Published

DirectRepeater: Genome Assembly Annotation

R package for annotating direct repeats in genome assemblies, providing tools for structural variant analysis and genome characterization.

R Package Genome Assembly
Published

Orphan Gene Evolution

Investigating evolutionary origins and characteristics of orphan genes, challenging the notion they represent a distinct biological entity.

Gene Evolution Comparative Genomics
