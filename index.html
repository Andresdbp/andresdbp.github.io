About

Hi, I'm Andres.

I recently earned my Ph.D. in Genetics & Genomics from Texas A&M University, where I studied genome evolution, population genetics, and sex-chromosome dynamics through computational and theoretical approaches.

Now I'm bringing that curiosity into the classroom and onto the pool deck. I teach science at Strake Jesuit College Preparatory and coach swimming — helping students think like scientists, ask good questions, and grow as people.

This site is where I share a little about myself and the research I'm proud of. For the full story, take a look at my CV.
